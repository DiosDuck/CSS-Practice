About me

Hi, I'm Țăpuc Mihai-Ovidiu — feel free to call me by either name. I was born on September 28, 1999, in Piatra-Neamț, and I'm currently living in Cluj-Napoca. I studied Computer Science and graduated from UBB with a degree in Mathematics and Computer Science.

I've always had a technical mindset and was really into Math and Physics during school. In high school, that interest shifted toward coding — I loved the idea of building something step by step. After university, I started working as a PHP developer and stuck with it because it's flexible and easy to work with, especially for web applications.

Outside of work, I enjoy skating and jogging (especially when the weather's nice), listening to rock music, and playing strategy games. I also like diving into odd bugs, especially in video games, just to figure out what caused them and how they could be fixed — it's like solving a mystery.

My goals as a programmer are to constantly explore new things and share my knowledge with others. No matter how much you think you know about a domain or language, there's always something new to learn—something that can be applied to your day-to-day projects and activities.
