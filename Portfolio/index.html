About me

Hello, my name is Ţăpuc Mihai-Ovidiu, I usually respond to both names. I was born on 28th September 1999 in Piatra-Neamţ and now I live in Cluj-Napoca. I studied Computer Science and graduated UBB in Mathematics and Computer Science.

At school I always had a tehnical mind, being passioned about Math and Physics. Once I started high school, my passion shifted into coding, building an application step by step. After University I started working on as PHP developer and sticked with it because of how easy and flexible it is to integrate into a web application.

Other than coding, I like jogging when the weather is nice outside, listening to rock and playing strategy games. Sometimes I like taking a deep look into weird bugs which occure (especially in video games), and try to understand what caused it and how it can be fixed or could have been avoided.
